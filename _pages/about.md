I am Emon, a researcher and engineer with a strong academic interest in machine learning, deep learning, and modern computing systems.

My work focuses on developing data-driven methods that are both theoretically grounded and practically useful. I am particularly interested in representation learning, model robustness, optimization, and the application of ML/DL to real-world problems across software and information systems.

Alongside research, I build reliable tools and reproducible workflows that support experimentation, evaluation, and deployment. I value clarity, rigor, and open scientific practice, and I actively seek opportunities where research can translate into measurable technological impact.

This website presents my publications, projects, and ongoing work in AI and related technologies.
